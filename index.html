About Me

I am a mechanical engineer and acoustics researcher focused on transcranial focused ultrasound, acoustic holography, and simulation-driven medical device design. My work combines numerical modeling (k-Wave, COMSOL, mSOUND), CT/MRI-based neuroimaging, and 3D-printed holographic lenses with hydrophone-based experimental validation.

Selected Projects

A sample of research, engineering, and personal projects. More details available on request.

Acoustic Holography & Transcranial Ultrasound

Experimental and computational investigation of elastic and acoustic wave propagation in layered media for transcranial focused ultrasound, integrating simulation, fabrication, and 3D hydrophone-based validation.

  • Built k-Wave and mSOUND models for skull and multilayer holographic lenses.
  • Used optimization to synthesize arbitrary and volumetric target patterns through heterogeneous bone.
  • Integrated COMSOL-derived stress tensors with angular spectrum methods to study coupled modes.

Skull-Conformal Acoustic Holographic Lenses

Patient-specific, skull-conformal acoustic holographic lenses to enhance transcranial therapeutic ultrasound by improving targeting accuracy and energy delivery through complex cranial geometries.

  • Generated conformal phase maps from CT/MRI-derived skull morphology.
  • Fabricated multiplex SLA-printed holograms tailored to individual skulls.
  • Validated focusing accuracy and mode-coupling with 3D hydrophone scans.

Class I Medical Device Design & Prototyping

Design and prototyping of Class I medical devices under NDA constraints, from CAD through fabrication-ready prototypes and design-for-manufacturing considerations.

  • Developed CAD models and design documentation for regulatory-compliant prototypes.
  • Coordinated with manufacturing partners to refine form, fit, and function.

Non-Pneumatic Tire with Skewed Hexagonal Honeycomb

Novel NPT concept using skewed hexagonal honeycomb structures to reduce contact pressure and rolling resistance while enhancing convective brake cooling.

  • Performed FEA and CFD (ANSYS Fluent) on rotating tire geometries.
  • Modeled heat transfer over brake rotors to evaluate cooling performance.
  • Identified an optimal skewness range balancing strength and heat rejection.
ASME IMECE paper

Active Magnetic Bearing: Design, Analysis & Test Bench

Contactless bearing system using electromagnetic levitation to eliminate mechanical wear and enable ultra-high rotational speeds.

  • Designed multi-pole stator geometry with independently controlled poles.
  • Developed models for force–current relationships and magnetic stiffness.
  • Used FEM to study flux, saturation, and actuator efficiency.
